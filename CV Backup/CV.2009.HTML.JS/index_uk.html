Philippe FOUQUE

Network Architect - LAN & WAN IP Specialist


I am currently a Network Architect and LAN & WAN IP Specialist at IBM France, within IGS/NDSC entity. My responsabilities are architecture, engineering, delivery, commissioning, provisioning and troubleshooting of customers' network projects such as new architectures or migrations, based on several multi-platform technologies like Cisco Systems and Checkpoint.

Previously, I was a network engineer at Nortel Networks EMEA , which allowed me to get a background of expert in the fields of private and public data networks as well as to specialise myself with data network products from Nortel's and Juniper Networks' catalog. My functions were consulting, design, engineering, delivery and troubleshooting for large customer accounts like, for instance, Carrier One, Orange UK, Utfors, Telefonica, BT Spain.
